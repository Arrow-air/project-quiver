# Project Description

Due to the new replacement of two altitude sensors, and the complexity of wiring operations of previous design. The whole altitude sensor mount has been redesigned to fit the installation of new sensor modules. 

Meanwhile, the location of the SIYI gimbal camera module has been mirrored to the right side of the aircraft, but the installation method remains the same as previous design.

# Methodology

The upper part of the new sensor mount has been design into an X-patterned structure for better weight reduction and 3D printing slicing, also for reaching both battery wall structures for screw fastening.

The screw insert bases of forward facing radar are also using large chamfer for non-support 3D printing.

![](images/Mounts/altitude_sensor_mount.png)

# Results and Deliverables 

The result is the single piece of new 3D printable altitude sensor mount. Screw inserts are required for the use. Also the patterned structure has been found benefiting the organizing the altitude sensor wiring.
